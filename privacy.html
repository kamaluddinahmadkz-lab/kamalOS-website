Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: kamalOS is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe in transparency and giving you control over your data.

Our Privacy Principles

kamalOS is built on these core privacy principles:

  • No Tracking: We don't track your activities or collect personal data
  • No Telemetry: No usage statistics are sent to our servers
  • Open Source: Our code is open for anyone to inspect
  • Your Data Stays Local: Everything you do stays on your computer

Information We Don't Collect

kamalOS does NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Usage statistics or telemetry data
  • File contents or browsing history
  • Location data
  • Crash reports (unless you manually submit them)
  • Analytics or tracking cookies

Website and Downloads

Website Visitors

Our website (kamalos.org) may collect:

  • Basic server logs (IP address, browser type, access time)
  • These logs are kept for 30 days for security purposes only
  • We don't use cookies or tracking scripts

ISO Downloads

When you download kamalOS:

  • We may count downloads for statistics
  • No personal information is required or collected
  • Downloads are provided directly without registration

Bug Reports and Support

If you submit a bug report or support request:

  • We collect only the information you voluntarily provide
  • This may include your email, system specs, and bug description
  • This information is used only to respond to your request
  • We don't sell or share this information with third parties
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

Third-Party Software

kamalOS includes software from various sources:

  • Ubuntu packages follow Ubuntu's privacy policy
  • Each software package has its own privacy policy
  • We recommend reviewing individual software privacy policies
  • kamalOS itself doesn't add tracking to third-party software

Data Security

Your security is important to us:

  • All data stays on your local computer
  • System updates use encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • We follow security best practices in our code
  • Security updates are released promptly

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use kamalOS without creating an account
  • Inspect our source code on GitHub
  • Modify the operating system as you wish
  • Remove or disable any features you don't want
  • Request deletion of any data you've submitted to us

Children's Privacy

kamalOS is safe for users of all ages. We don't knowingly collect information from children. Since we don't collect personal data from anyone, this applies to all users regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@kamalos.org
  • GitHub: github.com/kamalos/kamalos
  • Forum: forum.kamalos.org

Bottom Line: kamalOS respects your privacy. We don't collect, track, or sell your data. What you do on your computer stays on your computer.
